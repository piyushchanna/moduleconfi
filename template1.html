bhangarh

The Bhangarh Fort is a 17th-century fort built in the Rajasthan state of India.[1] It was built by Man Singh I (one of the navratans of Akbar's court) for his grandson Madho Singh I. It was named by Madho Singh after his grandfather Man Singh or Bhan Singh.A new village of Bhangarh with a population of 1,306 in 200 households has developed outside the limits of the fort as there is fear of ghosts haunting the old city. The fort and its precincts are well preserved
